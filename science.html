Scientific Questions

SWOT mission will provide observations of sea surface height and surface roughness at scales < 100 km, therefore opening the way for the systematic study of ocean turbulence down to the sub-mesoscales. Our NATL60 simulations will be key for designing and evaluating inversion methods expected to yield a breakthrough in our understanding of :

  • energy pathways toward small scales in the global ocean
  • the vertical exchanges between the surface layers and the ocean interior
