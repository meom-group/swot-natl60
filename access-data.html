How to access NATL60 data

If you want to collaborate in making science with NATL60 simulations, you are welcome !

First, begin with look at our outputs strategy .

Then, send us a mail with specifications for your needs : geographical region(s), period, variables and/or diagnostics ...

Last, 3 ways to access data :

  • filtering along satellite swath
  • gridded products to be integrated in assimilation systems
  • the retrieval algorithms for vertical velocities reconstruction
