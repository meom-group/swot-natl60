How to access NATL60 data

If you want to collaborate in making science with NATL60 simulations, you are welcome !

First, begin with look at our outputs strategy .

Then, send us a mail with specifications for your needs : geographical region(s), period, variables and/or diagnostics ...

In practice, depending on the data volumetry, there are three ways to get access to NATL60 data :

  • small fraction of NATL60 data can be accessed remotely through MEOM opendap channel
  • medium size datasets can be analyzed or downloaded through ssh/scp on our data server in Grenoble
  • for large volumetry, we ship data disks to our collaborators.
