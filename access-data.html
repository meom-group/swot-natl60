How to access NATL60 data

First, have a look at the outputs of our simulations.

Then, send us a mail with specifications for your needs :

  • geographical region(s)
  • period
  • variables

Last, 3 ways to access data :

  • direct access to the french scientific computing centers where data are stored when possible (CINES or CCRT)
  • extractions of medium and small size data available through opendap software
  • a storage disk can be sent to your location
