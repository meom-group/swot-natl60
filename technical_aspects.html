Technical aspects

The first run NATL60-MJM155 (following Drakkar classification) has been produced on Occigen supercomputer between December, 2014 and February, 2015.

It has consummed over 18 millions hours of CPU !

Generalities

For this run, the NEMO3.5_beta ocean model has been coupled to LIM2 ice model and has been forced by DFS5.2 atmospheric inputs.

The years 2008 and 2009 are available, after a spin-up phase of 2 years.

Outputs

We defined some sub-domains and sections across the North Atlantic basin, in which outputs will be more frequent than for the whole domain.

Based on this hierarchy of outputs we can provide any extraction you'll need !
